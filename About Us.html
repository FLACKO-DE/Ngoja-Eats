About Ngoja Eats

Our Journey

Welcome to Ngoja Eats, where every bite tells a story and every moment is a celebration of flavour, community, and passion.

Our journey began with a simple dream. Founder Bonokuhle Tshabalala started cooking at a young age with his mom and fell in love with it. He wanted to share the delicious food he grew up enjoying. The name “Ngoja Eats” reflects two of his passions—food and friendship—'Ngoja' comes from his friend group.

At Ngoja Eats, we believe food is more than nourishment—it's a bridge that brings people together. From the carefully curated menu to the warm hospitality, our values are quality, authenticity, and connection.

Our Food

Every dish is made with love using the freshest locally sourced ingredients. Whether you're enjoying one of our signature dishes like The Hub Burger or discovering a new favourite, we aim to make every visit unforgettable.
